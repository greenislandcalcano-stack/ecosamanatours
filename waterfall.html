Adventure in the Rainforest

El Limón Waterfall Tour

Trek or ride horseback through lush jungle trails to reach one of the most breathtaking waterfalls in the Caribbean.

Book Now Chat on WhatsApp

Overview

This guided eco-tour takes you through tropical forests and cocoa plantations to the stunning 50-meter El Limón waterfall — where you can swim in its natural pool.

Itinerary

  • Transfer from your hotel to the trailhead.
  • Horseback ride or guided hike through the forest.
  • Stop for tropical fruit tasting.
  • Arrival and swim at the waterfall.

What’s Included

  • Transportation & guide
  • Entrance fees
  • Horse rental (optional)
  • Snacks or local lunch

Key Info

  • Duration: Half day
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Location: El Limón, Samaná
  • Includes: Guide & equipment
