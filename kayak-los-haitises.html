A Peaceful Adventure Inside Los Haitises

This kayak experience is perfect for families, couples, nature lovers, and travelers who want to explore Los Haitises National Park in a calm, intimate, and unforgettable way. Instead of rushing through the bay, you will glide slowly between mangrove tunnels, tropical birds, hidden corners, and the dramatic limestone formations that make this park one of the most magical places in the Dominican Republic.
