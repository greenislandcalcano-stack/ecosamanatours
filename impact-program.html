EcoSamana Impact Program

The EcoSamana Impact Program is the official corporate social responsibility initiative of EcoSamana Adventures EIRL. Its purpose is to transform a portion of the tourism activity into real, accountable, and sustainable benefits for rural communities in Saman√° ‚Äî through social support and community sports development.

1. Vision & Mission

Vision

To build a sustainable bridge between responsible tourism and rural families in Saman√°, creating opportunities, support, and hope within the communities we visit.

Mission

To channel voluntary contributions from travelers and partners into concrete social and sports development projects, ensuring transparency, measurable results, and active community participation.

2. Program Structure

The EcoSamana Impact Program is built around two complementary components:

  • EcoSamana CARE ‚Äì Direct support for vulnerable rural families.
  • EcoSamana SPORTS ‚Äì Sports and recreational development in rural communities.

3. EcoSamana CARE

Objective: Improve the quality of life of rural families in the communities connected to our excursions, giving priority to those in greatest need.

Focus Areas

  • Food and essential household items.
  • School supplies (backpacks, notebooks, uniforms, shoes).
  • Basic medication and hygiene products.
  • Small home repairs (roof leaks, paint, simple restorations).
  • Emergency assistance for critical situations.

Suggested funding model (once active):
Guests will have the option to contribute at checkout or during the excursion with suggested amounts (US$2, US$5, US$10+). Contributions will be optional, clearly labeled as donations, and managed in a transparent, separated fund.

4. EcoSamana SPORTS

Objective: Promote healthy recreation, youth development, and community integration through sports facilities and local athletics support.

Focus Areas

  • Building or repairing simple multi-use courts (basketball/volleyball).
  • Providing sports gear: balls, nets, uniforms, shoes.
  • Supporting school and community sports activities.
  • Hosting friendly tournaments and youth engagement events.

Suggested funding model (once active):
Guests may choose to contribute US$3 or more toward the SPORTS fund, while hotels, donors, and corporate sponsors may support individual projects.

5. Management & Transparency

The program will be managed by EcoSamana Adventures EIRL, maintaining separate accounting records for contributions and project expenses.

Transparency Mechanisms

  • Quarterly reports listing total contributions and total spending per category.
  • Documentation of supported families or completed sports projects.
  • Photo evidence and progress tracking (always respecting privacy and dignity).
  • Reports available for MITUR, community leaders, hotels, and project sponsors.

6. Community Participation

Community leaders, school directors, rural representatives, and local councils will help identify priority needs and monitor project execution. The program‚Äôs structure is designed to be respectful, collaborative, and culturally aligned.

7. Potential Partners

Ideal partners for the EcoSamana Impact Program include:

  • Ministry of Tourism (MITUR)
  • Local schools & INEFI
  • Municipal governments & district councils
  • Hotels, guesthouses, and excursion operators
  • Private foundations & NGOs
  • Local companies interested in CSR initiatives

8. Current Status

The program is currently in its development and consultation stage. Once approvals and partnerships are finalized, EcoSamana Adventures will publish the official launch, activate donation mechanisms, and announce the first community pilot project.
