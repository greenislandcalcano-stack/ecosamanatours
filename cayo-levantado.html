Cayo Levantado Day Pass – Relaxed Beach Time in Samaná Bay

Cayo Levantado, also known by many visitors as Bacardi Island, is one of the best-known island spots in Samaná Bay. This experience is designed for travelers who want a simple, beautiful day on the water without long transfers or a rushed schedule.

After the boat ride from Samaná, you’ll have time to enjoy the beach, swim in calm turquoise water, walk the shoreline, take photos, and enjoy the tropical setting at your own pace. It’s a great option for couples, families, and small groups looking for an easy and scenic half-day or day experience.

What the Experience Is Really Like

This is not a high-adrenaline excursion. It is a smooth and scenic island outing made for people who want to enjoy the beauty of Samaná Bay in a more relaxed way.

The boat ride is short and pleasant, and once on the island, most guests spend their time swimming, resting under palm trees, enjoying the sand, taking photos, and soaking in the tropical atmosphere. It’s one of the easiest excursions to enjoy if you want something beautiful without a complicated itinerary.

Depending on the day and the season, the beach atmosphere may feel lively or peaceful, but the main appeal remains the same: easy island access, clear water, beautiful views, and a classic Caribbean setting.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the trip?

It depends on the package and timing, but most guests enjoy this as a half-day to day experience including boat transfer and beach time.

Is this good for children and older guests?

Yes. This is one of the easier and more relaxed excursions in Samaná, which makes it a good choice for families and mixed-age groups.

Is the island always quiet?

The atmosphere can vary depending on the day, season, and visitor volume. Some days feel peaceful and laid-back, while others are livelier.

Can I book a private boat?

Yes. Private boat service is available from US$600 for groups up to 10 people, subject to availability.

What if weather conditions change?

Boat operations depend on safe conditions in the bay. If weather affects the trip, we coordinate the best available solution directly with you.
