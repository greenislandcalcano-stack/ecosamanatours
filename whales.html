Overview

Every winter, thousands of humpback whales migrate to Samaná Bay to mate and give birth. Join our certified eco-guides for a respectful, close encounter with these gentle giants.

Highlights

  • Guided by certified marine biologists.
  • Guaranteed whale sightings (Jan–Mar).
  • Educational commentary about whale behavior.
  • Panoramic views of Samaná Bay and its islets.

Safety & Sustainability

We strictly follow conservation guidelines: safe distances, no engine revving near pods, and zero feeding or touching — to ensure minimal stress on the whales.

Key Info

  • Season: January – March
  • Duration: 3–4 hours
  • Location: Samaná Bay
  • Includes: Snacks & drinks

What to Bring

  • Hat & sunglasses
  • Camera / binoculars
  • Light clothing
  • Eco-sunscreen
