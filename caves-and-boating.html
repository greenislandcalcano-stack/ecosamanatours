Overview

This tour combines adventure and history as we navigate hidden mangrove channels and enter prehistoric caves featuring ancient Taíno art.

Itinerary

  • Depart from Samaná pier by boat.
  • Visit mangrove forest and birdwatching areas.
  • Explore caves with ancient rock carvings.
  • Swim or relax at a secret beach.
