Overview

This half-day adventure is perfect for travelers who want to experience the magic of Los Haitises National Park without spending the whole day on the water. Because the departure is from Sánchez, the crossing is shorter both ways, making the tour more comfortable, efficient, and family-friendly.

You’ll navigate through mangroves, discover dramatic limestone scenery, visit two caves, and enjoy a gentle trail through bosque húmedo before returning to Sánchez, where a tasty local lunch awaits.

Why Choose This Half-Day Tour?

  • Departures from Sánchez for a faster and easier crossing.
  • Ideal for families with children and travelers who prefer shorter excursions.
  • More private feel and less time in transit.
  • Includes two cave visits and a humid forest trail.
  • Great option for family groups, celebrations, and special events.
  • No stop at Cayo Levantado — the focus stays on Los Haitises itself.

Typical Itinerary

  1. 9:00 AM: Departure from Sánchez by boat.
  2. Short crossing into Los Haitises National Park.
  3. Navigation through mangroves and limestone formations.
  4. Visit two caves with natural formations and Taíno heritage.
  5. Gentle trail through bosque húmedo.
  6. Return to Sánchez.
  7. Local lunch served back in Sánchez, with snacks and drinks included.
  8. 1:00 PM: End of experience.
