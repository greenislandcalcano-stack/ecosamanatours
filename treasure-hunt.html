Overview

Turn your day at sea into a real-life pirate story with one of the most original family excursions in Samaná. This exclusive EcoSamana experience combines easy snorkeling, playful treasure-hunt challenges, and relaxing beach time into one smooth half-day adventure.

Designed for families, couples, small groups, and cruise visitors, this tour offers much more than a simple boat trip. Guests become the crew, the bay becomes the map, and every stop feels like part of a lively tropical quest. Children love the clues, adults love the scenery, and everyone loves finding the treasure chest in the end.

Best of all, the experience is easy, safe, and fun — with calm snorkeling areas, beautiful coastal views, friendly guides, and the kind of atmosphere that turns a normal vacation day into one of the stories your family keeps telling long after the trip.

Itinerary (Typical)

  1. Pickup & boarding at the pier or hotel dock in Santa Bárbara de Samaná.
  2. Boat ride along the bay with panoramic views of the town, coastline, and nearby islands.
  3. Snorkeling stop at a calm, shallow area — perfect for kids and beginners. Life jackets and basic instruction provided.
  4. Pirate clues & beach games on shore: follow maps, solve clues, and complete simple challenges together as a team.
  5. Treasure chest reveal with small prizes or surprises for the group.
  6. Relaxing beach time for swimming, photos, and enjoying the scenery before returning to Samaná.
