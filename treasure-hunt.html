Treasure Hunt Tour

Family-friendly snorkeling, beach games, and a hidden chest to find!

Overview

An immersive, story-driven eco-adventure along Samaná’s shoreline. Follow pirate-style clues, snorkel in turquoise waters, and compete in a beach game that leads to a final treasure chest. Ideal for families, friends, and groups.

Itinerary (Typical)

  • Boat ride along the Santa Bárbara coastline
  • Snorkeling stop — gear provided
  • Clue #1 on a secluded beach (message in a bottle)
  • Clue #2 underwater capsule
  • Beach game + final clue → treasure chest reveal
  • Snacks & soft drinks included (or buffet for hotel version)

What’s Included

  • Boat, captain, and crew
  • Snorkeling gear (limited sizes available)
  • Snacks / soft drinks (or buffet depending on package)
  • Lifejackets for all ages

Safety Notes

  • No alcohol served onboard; family-friendly environment
  • No head-diving at restricted spots
  • Lifejackets required for kids
